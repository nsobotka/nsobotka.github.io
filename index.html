About Me

Hello! I'm Nathan, a PhD candidate in computer science at Stanford University, where I'm working with Professor Kunle Olukotun. My research interests are computer architecture, reconfigurable dataflow accelerators, and memory systems. Before I entered Stanford CS's PhD program in 2024, I attended the University of Pennsylvania, where I received my bachelor's and master's degrees in computer science.
