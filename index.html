Projects

  • The Vellvm Project. I am part of the team working on the memory model of the vellvm project. The project is available at https://github.com/vellvm.
  • Engl009 Project. A scraping of data from old Penn yearbooks from 1900 to 2000. This website presents interesting tidbits found in the yearbooks, and has useful information on how to proceed with gathering more data for anyone who may want to do a similar project in the future. The project was done for a digital humanities class.
  • Waffler. This project recreates the Waffle game with the purpose of determining the optimal solution to the daily Waffle. It also includes the ability to play unlimited boards, restart games, and try out the official daily waffle without fear of failure. Visit the Waffle Game to see and play the official game. Credit to @thatwafflegame for the game concept.

For more projects, please reach out to me at nsobotka@seas.upenn.edu. Depending on who you are, I may be able to share some of my other work that is not currently public.
